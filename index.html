Design Portfolio

Store Planning & Design • Interior Design • Marketing & Branding

Create stores that grow.

My goal is to support the growth of food co-ops and independent natural foods stores. Nowadays, that's harder than ever. With competition and corporate entities wanting their piece of the organic pie, stores are facing very real threats.

Store Planning & Layout

You don't need to break the mold to have an amazing store, people just have to WANT to shop your store! Let's make it easy for them to leave with a full basket. I also specialize in store equipment and can specify the latest and greatest for your store, making sure to utilize green practices.

Marketing, Branding & Design

Our values and principles make us different - so let's use them! In this competitive market, stores need to highlight why they rock. The interior design and signage you choose can make or break that image. Brand implementation is one of my favorite jobs - no matter who came up with the strategy. The HOW is what I love. We can have all the great ideas in the world but if we don't implement them they're still just great ideas.
